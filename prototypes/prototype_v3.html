How it all started

A little story time.

F

or those who want the whole story: it started on a Sunday in November 2023, when Joyce sent Ryan a message on Bumble about a video on his profile, an art installation he'd built for a skateboard pop-up at Art Basel. She thought he seemed cozy. Within a few exchanges, they were trading song recommendations, realizing how intertwined their taste in music was.

Three weeks later, they were wandering Balboa Park on a cool December night on their first date, chasing the international spirit garden and arriving just minutes too late. They ended up at a dive bar called Livewire instead.

The dates that followed only got better, each one more natural than the last. Italian food in Ocean Beach. A home-cooked dinner with the right music already on when he walked in. Then, at a record fair, Joyce put Ryan's diffing skills to work to fulfill her dream of finding Eddie Murphy's Party All the Time LP. Success!

Music was the thread running through all of it. Soul, funk, rare finds, the quest for the perfect record. Ryan's collection of a few thousand vinyls immediately dwarfed Joyce's growing but proud little stack. Ryan has been digging since he was a teenager. Joyce just has good taste, clearly. They fit.

Let's not forget a Miniature Bull Terrier named Kaj eventually weighed in too, firmly and loudly, in Ryan's favor.

They took their time, and the story keeps getting better.

"It's only forever, not long at all."
